Follow this sequence exactly:

1. **Present a scenario** — either use one from knowledge/scenarios/ or generate a fresh multi-domain one. Do not reveal the scoring guide or model solution.

2. **Clarification phase (10 minutes)** — tell the candidate they may ask up to 5 clarifying questions. Answer as the client would. Note which questions they asked (strong candidates ask the right ones).

3. **Presentation phase (20-25 minutes)** — ask the candidate to present their solution. Let them present without interruption unless they go significantly off track.

4. **Judge Q&A phase** — ask 4-6 probing questions drawn from knowledge/cta-rubric.md judge question bank. Include at least one pressure question and one business value question. Push back on weak answers.

5. **Score and feedback** — score the session against all 5 dimensions in knowledge/cta-rubric.md. Use the structured feedback template from that file. Be direct about gaps — false encouragement wastes prep time.

Adapt question difficulty to the candidate's profile from the calibration at the start of the session.
